Experimental — paper trading only. Voltra is in its validation phase. The current strategy's edge is not yet statistically significant (Monte Carlo P(edge>0) = 81%, below the 95% bar). It trades simulated money by default. Nothing here is financial advice; crypto trading can lose you money.

The process is the edge

Backtest (2020–2026)→ Walk-forward→ Monte Carlo→ 30-day dry-run→ Small live capital

Every stage is a gate. No stage is skipped. Fees (0.16% round-trip) and slippage (0.05%) are modeled in every backtest; results are validated out-of-sample and stress-tested across bull, bear, and chop regimes.

What's inside

⚡ Strategies

TrendBreak (structure breakouts) and a BTC/ETH→SOL cross-asset lead-lag model, with 1% ATR risk sizing, on-exchange stops, and drawdown circuit breakers.

🔬 Honest validation

Hyperopt on training data only, walk-forward out-of-sample, and a Monte Carlo edge-significance gate. Rejected ideas are documented, not hidden.

🛡️ Enterprise ops

Caddy TLS ingress, health monitoring with a live-mode tripwire, automated backups + tested restore, rate limiting, and an incident runbook.

🖥️ Desktop controller

A Tauri tray app that runs the whole Docker stack on its own with one click and a start-at-login toggle. Signed auto-updates.

🔌 TradingView bridge

An isolated experimental bot that can trade external Pine v6 webhook signals — still wrapped in Voltra's risk rules.

📊 WordPress monitor

A standards-clean plugin that collects time-series bot data, shows a dashboard, and exports CSV for analysis.
